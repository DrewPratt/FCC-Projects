A Timeline of Da Vinci's Life:

  • 1452 - Leonardo da Vinci is born near Florence, Italy. He was raised by his father.
  • 1467 - Leonardo is sent away to apprentice art under Andrea del Verrochio.
  • 1472 - Leonardo is asked to join The Painter's Guild of Florence.
  • 1477 - Leonardo out-paints his teacher on the joint painting "The Baptism of Christ".
  • 1482 - Da Vinci Moves from Florence to Milan, Italy.
  • 1490 - Sometime during the 1490's Da Vinci begins keeping notebooks full of his philosophies and beliefs.
  • 1495 - Da Vinci paints The Last Supper.
  • 1500 - Da Vinci returns to Florence.
  • 1502 - Leonardo accepts a position travelling with the military troops in order to sketch out the land and draw maps
  • 1503 - Da Vinci paints The Mona Lisa.
  • 1506 - Leonardo returns to Milan to work as an architect for the French governor.
  • 1513 - The French leave Milan. Leonardo goes to Rome.
  • 1516 - Leonardo is invited by the King of France and accepts. He lives the rst of his life in France.
  • 1519 - Leonardo da Vinci dies at 67 years old. He was buried in France.
