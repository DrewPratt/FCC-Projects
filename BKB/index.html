About

Box Knife Betty was a four piece rock band from Chico, CA. Formed in early 2013. Their sound is best described as blues influenced heavy rock. With angsty, topical lyrical content the band takes on the divisive issues of the day and challenges the status quo without taking themselves too seriously. Influences range from Led Zeppelin, Jimi Hendrix, and Eric Clapton to Black Sabbath, Clutch, and Helmet.

Shortly after recording their debut album, Monsters Among Us, in 2014 Box Knife Betty broke up and never released the record.
